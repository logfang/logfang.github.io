Biography

Xidong Mu is a Postdoctoral Researcher in School of Electronic Engineering and Computer Science at Queen Mary University of London (QMUL) , London, U.K. He received the Ph.D. and B.S. degrees from the Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) in 2022 and 2015, respectively. His research interests include non-orthogonal multiple access, IRSs/RISs aided communications, integrated sensing and communications (ISAC), and optimization theory.
